Community

Community plays a big role in Wellington College’s reputation and culture. A community is defined to be ‘a group of people living in the same place or having a particular characteristic in common’. At Wellington College, we embrace the students and teachers living around us.

Wellington College community is very close and has a very strong culture around the brotherhood that we embrace. What this does is brings all of the Wellington College boys closer as a school and as a community.

We encourage all students to get along no matter the colour, race or culture they believe in. Everyone in this world is different so we want the boys to leave this school with an open heart and accepting of no matter who they meet.
